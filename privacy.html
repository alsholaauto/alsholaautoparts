Privacy Policy

How Al Shola Auto Spare Parts collects, uses and protects the information you share when you contact us.

Last updated: 6 June 2026

1. Who We Are

Al Shola Auto Spare Parts LLC ("Al Shola", "we", "us") is a Dubai-licensed auto spare parts trader (Dubai Government Trade Licence No. 1560955) supplying genuine and aftermarket parts to car owners, workshops, fleet operators and exporters.

2. Information We Collect

We are a contact-and-quote business — we do not operate an online checkout. We only collect what you choose to send us:

  • Your name and contact details (phone, WhatsApp number or email) when you make an enquiry.
  • Vehicle details you provide — make, model, year, and VIN or chassis number — used solely to identify the correct part.
  • The content of your messages and order requests.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify part fitment against your vehicle and prepare an accurate quote.
  • To respond to your enquiry, confirm stock, and arrange supply or delivery.
  • To prepare invoices and export documentation for B2B and international orders.
  • To keep records required under UAE commercial and tax regulations.

4. WhatsApp & Third-Party Messaging

Enquiries sent through WhatsApp are handled within the WhatsApp platform and are subject to WhatsApp's own privacy terms. We use your number only to respond to your enquiry and provide service updates you request.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We share information only where necessary — for example with logistics and shipping partners to fulfil delivery or export, or where required by UAE law or competent authorities.

6. Data Retention & Security

We retain enquiry and transaction records only as long as needed for service and legal compliance, and apply reasonable measures to protect them against unauthorised access.

7. Your Rights

You may ask us to access, correct or delete the personal information you have shared, subject to records we must keep by law. Contact us using the details below.
